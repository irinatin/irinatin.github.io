My name is Irina Tin. I am originally from a small country that was a part of Soviet Union a long time ago. I moved to Pittsburgh on April's Fool Day in 2016. Before that I worked for one of the wealthiest philanthropists and businessmen of Russia for about 9 years.

When I moved to the US and after having odd jobs for about 18 months, I decided to pursue a career in real estate as I always liked the architecture and interior design. At some point I wanted to create an app and was inspired by the specialized software that we had been using at that time. However I was stuck on the back-end. The idea that you can create something from scratch using only your laptop, skills, and ideas, fascinated me so I decided that I wanted to shift into the software engineering field and started looking for ways how to get there.

I found out about the Tech Elevator Pittsburgh and graduated in April of 2020. They helped me to land a job as a Software Developer Associate and I'm very grateful for that!
